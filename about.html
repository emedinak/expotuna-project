Expotuna S.A is a frozen fish and seafood processing and marketing company founded in 2005, committed to continuous improvement in quality, food safety, and industrial security. Its products meet the highest standards, undergoing semi-annual validation by the National Fisheries Institute.

Located in Guayaquil near Ecuador's two main ports, the company benefits from reliable access to fresh seafood and efficient containerized shipping. A dedicated quality control team supervises every stage of production 24 hours a day, 365 days a year.
