Fleet of Ships

Expotuna S.A is a frozen fish and seafood processing and marketing enterprise that began operations in 2005. Since its inception, it has remained committed to continuous improvement in quality, food safety, and industrial security.

It adheres to the highest quality standards, with its products undergoing semi-annual validation by the National Fisheries Institute (INP) and annual certification under the BRC Global Standard for Food Safety.

Expotuna S.A is strategically located in Guayaquil, benefiting from proximity to Ecuador’s principal ports — Guayaquil and Manta.

A dedicated quality control team supervises every stage of production around the clock — 24 hours a day, 365 days a year.

The company has recently expanded its harvesting, production and storage capacities to meet growing international demand.

Delivery Fleet

Transportation and distribution are carried out daily using our dedicated nationwide fleet of refrigerated vehicles, ensuring the integrity of the cold chain and preserving the freshness and quality of our products throughout delivery.
