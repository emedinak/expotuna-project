ABOUT EXPOTUNA

Premium Ecuadorian Seafood Exporter

Expotuna S.A is a frozen fish and seafood processing and marketing company founded in 2005, committed to continuous improvement in quality, food safety, and industrial security. Its products meet the highest standards, undergoing semi-annual validation by the National Fisheries Institute (INP) and annual certification under the BRC Global Standard for Food Safety, consistently achieving an “A” rating.

Strategically located in Guayaquil near Ecuador’s two main ports—Guayaquil and Manta—the company benefits from reliable access to fresh seafood and efficient containerized shipping. A dedicated quality control team supervises every stage of production 24 hours a day, 365 days a year, ensuring strict monitoring from catch to processing, packaging, storage, and shipment, with full traceability and compliance with national and international inspections.

In recent years, Expotuna S.A has expanded its harvesting, production, and storage capacities, strengthening its ability to supply global markets with high-quality, competitively priced seafood products under its various brands.
